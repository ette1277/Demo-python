Emgity Ltd is a culture-centric community-based co-op that values the health of grass-root farmers. That are into daily farming, by providing farmers with basic health information. Which includes physical health observation and early interventions to support grassroot farmers seeking medical help when needed.

We leverage on grassroot farming, by providing them with different farming training and technology from around the world. We use Agricultural Technology (AgriTech) to increase productivity farming for grassroot farmers, and provide information on Insurance Technology Insurance Technology (InsurTech) deploying AI and IoT to create optimal value for our members.
